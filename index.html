📖 How to Play

⚛ The Basics
Tap the reactor core to generate energy. Energy is your currency — use it to buy generators that produce energy automatically every second.
🛸 Generators
Buy generators in the Generators tab. Each type produces more energy per second but costs more. Owned generators orbit the reactor as glowing icons. Stack multiples for higher output.
⚡ Boosts
One-time permanent upgrades in the Boosts tab. Click multipliers increase your tap power. Generator multipliers scale all passive income. Active boosts show as green pills below your stats.
🏆 Trophies
Achievements unlock automatically as you hit milestones (total clicks, energy earned, generators owned). Each trophy grants a permanent bonus — shown as gold pills in the HUD.
🔮 Prestige & Ascension
Once you've earned enough total energy (1M+), you can Prestige to reset your energy and generators in exchange for Plasma Cores.
Each Plasma Core gives a +10% permanent multiplier to ALL production and click power. This stacks — 10 cores = 2x everything, 50 cores = 6x everything.
Your boosts and trophies survive prestige. The formula is: Cores = √(total energy ÷ 1,000,000). The more you earn before prestiging, the more cores you get.
💡 Tip: Wait until you can get at least 5–10 cores before your first prestige. Early resets for 1–2 cores aren't worth it.
☁️ Cloud Saves
Create an account (👤 button) to sync progress across devices. Your save auto-syncs every 30 seconds and when you close the tab. You can also export your save as text from Settings → Save & Load.
